What is HCHS Share?


HCHCS Share is a web app made by Emmett Bicker, a HCHS student, for sharing notes.

Because of the COVID-19 pandemic, I noticed that many students were out from school sick. I noticed that because classes are usually with different people in high school, it's harder to get notes for every class. To help absent students keep up with school work, I've made this web app where students can both upload and see notes.



Upload

You can upload your notes by clicking the Upload button on the navbar. It would be wonderful if you could upload your class notes here!











View

Through the above search bar, you can search all notes uploaded to this website by using a teacher's name and period to search! If there are no notes for that specific class, I suggest looking at other classes taught by the same teacher. A friend of mine gave me an google sheet of the classes which I modified a little to work with the site. You can look through the spreadsheet and find what other classes your teacher teaches!



Notice

This is only for notes. Homework is not allowed on this site, please don't upload it. If you do, it and your account will be removed.

This site is also not affiliated with HCHS admin/teachers. They can certainly use it, but they are not responsible for anything that happens on here. For any issues with the site you can email me at emmettbicker@hunterschools.org
