Me!


I'm Emmett Bicker, a sophomore at HCHS. I made this website for three main reasons:

  • So many people have COVID-19 in the school, I realized that there wasn't much way for the students to keep up with course material. This seemed like at least a partial solution for the problem of not being able to know what was going on in class.

  • This is my final project for Harvard's course CS50! David J. Malan, the instructor, taught me how to do all of this and the final project's description was to make something impactful. This was the most impactful thing I could think of doing, and I thought the best way to repay the great instruction of the course was to do the most impactful thing I could.

  • It's fun! Computer Science is super fun, I made all of this out of basically text files and it has the potential to be a popular file sharing system that might actually impact people's lives. In my eyes that's amazing and I want to do more stuff like this in the future (probably not web applications for a bit though one is enough for the next 6 months)! I hope other people at Hunter get more involved in computer science, I only started 6 months ago so anyone can start at any time and make something like this!
I hope this website can make your school life somewhat easier!

If in the worst situation that literally no one uploads notes to this website, I will upload mine! If you're in any of my classes, my notes will be in the respective place on the site by 4PM the day of.

Finally I encourage everyone to take CS50. It's a fantastic course and it has really deepened my understanding of computer science. Here's the certificate I got from submitting this as my final project.
