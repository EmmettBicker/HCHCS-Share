What is HCHS Share?


HCHCS Share is a web app made by Emmett Bicker, a HCHS student, for sharing notes.

Because of the COVID-19 pandemic, I noticed that many students were out from school sick. To help absent students keep up with school work, I've made this web app where students can both upload and see notes.
